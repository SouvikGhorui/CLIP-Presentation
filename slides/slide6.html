Full CLIP Objective

$$\mathcal{L} = -\frac{1}{2N}\sum_{i=1}^{N}\left[\log\frac{e^{s_{ii}/\tau}}{\sum_k e^{s_{ik}/\tau}} + \log\frac{e^{s_{ii}/\tau}}{\sum_k e^{s_{ki}/\tau}}\right]$$

where $s_{ij} = \mathbf{I}_i^{\top}\mathbf{T}_j$ is the cosine similarity and $\tau$ is the learned temperature.
